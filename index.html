I Like Cats!

I used to be a dog person but now I love both cats and dogs equally. Maybe I even like cats a little bit more than dogs. Cats are amazing animals that have been companions to humans for thousands of years. They are independent, curios, and playful. Some cats are very social and love attention, while others prefer to explore quietly on their own.

One of the reasons I like cats is because they are very unique. Each cat has its own personality. Some cats love to cuddle, while others are more independent and like to be alone. Cats are also very clean animals and spend a lot of time licking themselves.
